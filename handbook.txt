Company Leave Policy

Employees are entitled to 15 paid vacation days per year, accrued monthly.
Sick leave is capped at 10 days annually and does not roll over.
New employees must complete a 90-day probation period before requesting vacation.
Remote work is permitted up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
The office is closed on all national holidays plus December 24th through January 2nd.

Expense Policy

Employees can expense meals up to $40 per day while traveling for work.
All expenses over $200 require pre-approval from a department head.
Reimbursements are processed within 10 business days of submission.
